What Exactly is Incube Club ?

Incube is a leadership and entrepreneurship facility with the "Initiate, Innovate and Inspire" to encourage future entrepreneus to give capacity to innovate. Design and Develop new technological solutions to challenging and ever changing needs of the society.

Our Vision

To be a platform for evolution of future entrepreneurs capable to innovate, design and develop new technological solutions to challenging and ever changing needs of society.

Our Mission

To inculcate entrepreneur skills such as problem solving, team work,management and strategic decision-making skills among students through interaction with industry experts,workshops, bootcamps and skill development sessions.
