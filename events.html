Talent Pool

Talent pool is a four week entrepreneur development program in which students with start-up ideas were given an opportunity to present and develop their ideas and also new problem statements were given to students without ideas. Students were assigned with mentors to assist them in their projects. The major goal of this program is to offer internships to students with finest project ideas and execution. .
